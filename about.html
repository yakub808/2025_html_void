Jakub Pietryszyn is an architectural engineer specialized in space architecture, 3D modeling, and related software workflows.

He focuses on concept development, 3D modeling, and system-level design, integrating appropiate technologies
to support adaptable living environments in extreme conditions - from habitat architecture to space mission scenario studies and visualization.

Jakub's journey in architecture has been enriched with 10+ years of experience, through collaborations with esteemed studios such as MAZM, KAPS, Studium and Konopka Engineering.
This experience has allowed him to contribute to a diverse array of projects, ranging from private residences and public buildings
to heavy industry and commercial establishments.

Jakub collaborates with research stations, innovation institutes, studios, and media partners on habitat design,
mission scenario visualization, and software-enabled space architecture workflows.
